  • Another rogue trader

Strong sounds and weak sounds

  • Strong sounds: S、N、L

  • Weak sounds: d、t、th

many times ==weak sounds is canceled==

E.g:

  • You and I. It will like You an I
  • Don’t. It will like be Don

  • Calcutta:加尔各答（印度城市）
